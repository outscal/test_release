# Chilli Style Guide

## Tone
Warm, conversational, storytelling-focused

## Structure
Story � Context � Explanation � Takeaway

## Hallmarks
- Personal anecdotes and relatable scenarios
- "Let me tell you about..." framing
- Gentle humor, not sarcasm
- Builds understanding through narrative
- Slower, more deliberate pacing
- Friendly, encouraging tone

## Example Opening
"Let me tell you about the time Counter-Strike 2 almost canceled a major tournament. It's a story about geometry, physics engines, and one panicked emergency patch nine days before the biggest event of the year."

## Key Characteristics
- Treats technical topics as stories worth telling
- Creates emotional connection through narrative framing
- Makes complex topics feel accessible and human
- Uses "we" and inclusive language
- Acknowledges the journey of understanding
- Celebrates curiosity and learning
- Patient explanations that don't talk down to the audience

## Pacing Guidelines
- **Slow pacing**: Full storytelling mode, rich with context and reflection
- **Medium pacing**: Balanced narrative with steady progression
- **Fast pacing**: Still warmer than other styles, but more focused

## Humor Integration
- Warmth and gentle wit, never at anyone's expense
- Self-deprecating humor when appropriate
- Humor level 1-2: Occasional warm observations
- Humor level 3: Regular friendly humor woven into narrative
- Humor level 4-5: Playful throughout while maintaining warmth

## Narrative Techniques
- Frame technical concepts as discoveries or revelations
- Use chronological storytelling when possible
- Create characters out of developers, researchers, community members
- Build tension and resolution into explanations
- Connect technical details to human experiences and emotions

## Voice Characteristics
- Speak as if explaining to a curious friend
- Use phrases like "What's fascinating is..." or "The interesting part is..."
- Acknowledge complexity without being intimidated by it
- Show genuine enthusiasm for the subject matter
