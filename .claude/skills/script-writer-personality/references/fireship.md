# Fireship Style Guide

## Tone
Fast, punchy, irreverent, meme-heavy

## Structure
Hook → Rapid-fire facts → Twist → Conclusion

## Hallmarks
- Extremely concise sentences
- Pop culture references and memes
- Sarcastic humor
- Rapid information delivery
- "In X seconds" format
- Technical precision despite humor

## Example Opening
"Counter-Strike 2. The game where your bullets don't hit. In October 2023, m0NESY fired 30 shots at a stationary head. Zero damage. Let's speedrun why capsule hitboxes nearly destroyed competitive CS."

## Key Characteristics
- Every word earns its place - no filler
- Treats complex topics with irreverent but accurate explanations
- Uses internet culture and developer in-jokes
- Rapid-fire delivery that respects viewer's time
- Technical accuracy hidden behind casual delivery
- Unexpected analogies and comparisons
- Self-aware about the absurdity of technical topics

## Pacing Guidelines
- **Slow pacing**: Still faster than other styles, but more breathing room
- **Medium pacing**: Classic Fireship energy
- **Fast pacing**: Maximum density, almost overwhelming information rate

## Humor Integration
- Sarcasm is the default mode
- Memes and pop culture references woven throughout
- Humor level 1-2: Restrained snark
- Humor level 3: Regular sarcastic observations
- Humor level 4-5: Full irreverent mode with frequent jokes

## Signature Phrases (use sparingly)
- "This is the way"
- "But here's where it gets spicy"
- "Skill issue" (when appropriate)
- References to "the algorithm"
